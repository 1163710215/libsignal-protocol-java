Class Overview

SessionBuilder is responsible for setting up encrypted sessions. Once a session has been established, SessionCipher can be used to encrypt/decrypt messages in that session.

Sessions are built from one of three different possible vectors:

  1. A PreKeyBundle retrieved from a server.
  2. A PreKeyWhisperMessage received from a client.
  3. A KeyExchangeMessage sent to or received from a client.
Sessions are constructed per recipientId + deviceId tuple. Remote logical users are identified by their recipientId, and each logical recipientId can have multiple physical devices.
